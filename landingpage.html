About

The UNT Food Finder is a web application designed to help University of North Texas students easily discover food options both on and off-campus. With customizable filters, users can explore restaurants, cafes, and eateries that meet their dietary preferences and restrictions, making the process of finding the perfect meal easier than ever.

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform that not only helps students find delicious food but also supports their individual dietary needs and preferences, enhancing their overall dining experience at UNT.
